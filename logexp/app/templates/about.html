About LogExp

LogExp is a Flask + Postgres application designed to ingest and display Geiger counter readings.

It integrates hardware via USB‑serial, stores readings in a structured database, and exposes both API endpoints and background services for continuous monitoring.

Created with a focus on reproducibility, onboarding clarity, and compliance‑driven architecture.
